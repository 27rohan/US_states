  • Population represents the population (in 1000s) of a state as of July 1, 1975

  • Income represents the per capita income (in 1974)

  • Illiteracy represents the illiteracy rate of individuals in a state (1970)

  • Life Exp represents the average life expectancy of an individual in a state (1969 to 1971)

  • Murder represents the murder and non-negligent manslaughter rate per 100,000 people in a state (1976)

  • HS Grad represents the high school graduation rate (in percentage) of children in a state (1970)

  • Frost represents the mean number of days with minimum temperature below freezing (1931 to 1960)

  • Area represents the total surface area of a state in square miles.
